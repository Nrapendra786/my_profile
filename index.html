About Richard

Richard Weißmann brings a unique perspective to his writing, drawing from over fifteen years of experience as a software engineer across multiple continents and industries. His work has taken him from Silicon Valley startups to European financial institutions and Asian tech hubs, providing him with a rich tapestry of experiences that inform his storytelling.

As an author, he combines his technical expertise with a passion for storytelling, creating narratives that explore the human side of technology and the impact of digital transformation on society. His writing often delves into themes of innovation, cultural exchange, and the ethical implications of advancing technology.
