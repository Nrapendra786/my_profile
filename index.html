The Silent Echo

"A masterpiece of contemporary literature that explores the depths of human connection."

In this compelling new novel, Richard Weißmann weaves a tale of mystery and intrigue set against the backdrop of modern-day Berlin. Following the journey of protagonist Marcus Schreiber, the story delves into themes of identity, memory, and redemption.

About Richard

Richard Weißmann is an award-winning author known for his intricate storytelling and deep character development. Born in Munich and raised in Berlin, his work often explores themes of identity, memory, and the complexity of human relationships.

With over a decade of writing experience, Richard has published five novels and numerous short stories. His work has been translated into twelve languages and has earned him several prestigious literary awards.
